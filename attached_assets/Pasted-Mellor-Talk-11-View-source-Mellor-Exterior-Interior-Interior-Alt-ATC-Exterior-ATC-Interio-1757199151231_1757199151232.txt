Mellor International or simply Mellor (ICAO: IMLR) is a medium sized airport located on the North West Island of Greater Rockford. It features a single runway (7-25), a thin passenger terminal with 4 gates, a singular helipad and hangar. The airport and terminal are based on the Bristol Airport an airport located 11 kilometers from Bristol, United Kingdom.
Prior to the May 18th, 2024 update, Mellor was fairly inactive due to its close proximity to the Old Greater Rockford Airport. The May 18th update increased the distance between the two airports with Mellor being located in the North West region whilst Greater Rockford Airport is located on the Eastern region.
